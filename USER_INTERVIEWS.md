# User Interviews

> **IMPORTANT FOR SUBMITTER:** This file contains interview templates and guidance.
> You MUST replace these with 3 real conversations before submitting.
> The assignment explicitly states fabricated interviews are an instant reject.
> Cold DM founders on X/Twitter, post in indie hacker Slacks, or ask your college network.
> Each conversation should be 10-15 minutes. Here's how to do it fast:

**How to get 3 interviews in 24 hours:**
1. Post in r/SideProject: "Building a free AI spend audit tool — anyone want to give 10min feedback? DM me"
2. DM 5 founders you know on LinkedIn or Twitter: "Hey, building something for eng teams — 10min chat?"
3. Ask a friend who works at any startup with >5 people

**Questions to ask:**
- "What AI tools does your team pay for?"
- "Do you know your total monthly spend off the top of your head?"
- "Have you ever audited or questioned those subscriptions?"
- "What would make you switch a tool you're currently using?"
- "Would a free audit tool be useful? What would you want it to tell you?"
