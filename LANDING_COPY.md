## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes, completely. No credit card, no trial period, no "free tier" with a wall. The audit is free because it's a lead-generation tool for Credex — we're upfront about that. If you have significant savings, we'll mention Credex as an option. If you don't, we'll tell you you're spending well.

**Q: What do you do with my data?**
A: Your tool and spend data is used only to generate your audit. If you submit your email, we store it to send you the report. We don't sell it, share it, or spam you. The public share URL strips your email and company name — only tool names and savings numbers are shown.

**Q: How accurate are the savings estimates?**
A: Every recommendation traces to an official vendor pricing page (documented in our PRICING_DATA.md). The logic is deterministic — not AI-generated guesses. A finance person should be able to verify every number. We cite our sources.

**Q: What if I'm already spending efficiently?**
A: We'll tell you. "You're spending well" is a valid and common output. We don't manufacture savings to look impressive — that would destroy trust. For users already optimized, we offer a "notify me when new optimizations apply" signup instead.

**Q: What is Credex?**
A: Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits — Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT Enterprise, and others — sourced from companies that over-forecasted usage or pivoted. The discount is real (20-35% off retail). SpendLens is a free tool we built to help teams find where they're overspending, and for users with large API bills, Credex is often part of the solution.
